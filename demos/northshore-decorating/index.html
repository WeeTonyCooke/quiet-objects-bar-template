Common questions

Things people ask.

Do you supply the paint?

Usually yes — trade-quality Colourtrend, Dulux Trade or Fleetwood, included in the price. Bring your own colours if you prefer.

Are quotes free?

Yes. Most rooms from photos. Bigger jobs get a free visit, no obligation.

Are you insured?

Public liability insurance. Certificate on request before work starts.

Do I need to move out?

No. Furniture covered, floors protected, room by room where possible.

How long does a room take?

Bedroom or sitting room: one to two days. Hall and stairs: two to three. Exteriors depend on the weather.

What about preparation?

Fill, sand, dust-free before the first coat. Skipping prep is what fails in eighteen months instead of lasting years.
